## 🛠 Step 2: The 12-Strategy Matrix (Proprietary Logic)
MarketBeacon scans every stock through 12 unique institutional lenses. To protect intellectual property, we use the following professional identifiers:

### 1. Velocity Retest (Formerly 20% Rally)
*   **Logic:** We find stocks that showed a "Burst of Power" (20% jump with consecutive green candles).
*   **The Wait:** We don't chase the high. We wait for the price to return to the **Base** (the price before the rally started).
*   **Entry:** Buy when price retests the origin level within 1 year.
*   **Goal:** Catch the second wave of institutional buying.

### 2. Deep Recovery Audit (Formerly 67 ka Funda)
*   **Logic:** Based on the observation that elite stocks often "reset" after a 67% fall from their All-Time High (ATH).
*   **Filter:** The stock MUST show improving quarterly financials (Sales/Profit) during this fall.
*   **Entry:** Buy when drawdown hits ~67% and fundamentals show a turnaround.

### 3. Institutional Floor (Formerly Envelope Long)
*   **Logic:** Institutional buyers often set a "floor" at the Lower Envelope (SMA 20 - 10%).
*   **Entry:** Buy when price touches or dips below the **Lower Band**.

### 4. Structural Pivot (Formerly Cup & Handle)
*   **Logic:** These look for classic chart patterns.
    *   **Cup & Handle:** Price forms a "U" shape followed by a small consolidation (handle).
*   **Entry:** Buy at the breakout of the "Neckline" or "Handle."

### 5. Dynamic Reversal (Formerly Reverse Head & Shoulders)
*   **Logic:** A sophisticated trend reversal pattern identification system.
*   **Entry:** Triggered upon completion of a structural "W" or inverted shoulder formation.

## 📊 Step 3: Fundamental Audit (The 100-Point Check)
Once you find a "Qualified" stock, click on it to see the **Fundamentals Page**.
*   **Ownership Matrix:** Look for **Smart Money Ownership > 75%**. This means Promoters, FIIs, and DIIs own the majority.
*   **Pledged Status:** Ensure this is **0%**. Avoid companies where promoters have mortgaged their shares.
*   **The Score:** Only take trades where the **Total Score is > 65/100**.

## 💰 Step 4: No-Stoploss Risk Management
Since we don't use stop-losses, we manage risk through **Tranches and Weightage**:

| Market Cap | Allocation | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Large Cap** | 50% of Capital | Safe, slow, steady entries. |
| **Mid Cap** | 30% of Capital | Higher growth potential. |
| **Small Cap** | 20% of Capital | Aggressive alpha generation. |

*   **Average Down (ABCD):** If a "Qualified" stock falls further, we add in 10% steps (Entry B, C, D) instead of exiting. We only buy companies we are happy to hold for years.
